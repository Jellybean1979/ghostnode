01OVERVIEW

GHOSTNODE is a browser-based hacker simulation game. You play as ghost_0x — an anonymous operator running darknet contracts for CipherCoins (CC). None of the hacking is real; all mechanics are thematic simulations.

Your goal: run contracts, earn CC, buy tools from the Black Market, unlock harder contracts, and climb the operator ranks from Novice all the way to Legend.

// NOTEProgress exists in your current browser session only. Refreshing resets everything.
02GAME LOOP

Every session follows the same three-phase loop:

PHASE 1ACCEPT CONTRACT
→
PHASE 2RUN MISSION
→
PHASE 3GET PAID
→
REPEATBUY TOOLS

Completing contracts earns CipherCoins and XP. XP raises your level and rank. CC is spent in the Black Market on tools that make missions faster, safer, or unlock harder tiers.

// TIPStart with easy Recon contracts to stack early CC, then invest in Ghost Proxy Mesh before attempting ★★★ or higher.
03THE SCREENS

Use the left sidebar to navigate. Here's what each screen does:

▶
TERMINAL
Home base. Boot sequence, ambient logs, 4 stat boxes, and a fake command line. Type help for commands.
◈
CONTRACTS
All available contracts. Locked cards need specific tools. Right sidebar shows your current stats and trace modifier.
⚠
MISSION
Auto-opens when a contract starts. Shows breach bar, trace meter, timer, terminal log, mini-game, and tool deploy buttons.
◆
BLACK MARKET
Underground tool shop. Browse all tools, see costs and effects. Click BUY to purchase — CC deducted immediately.
▣
INVENTORY
All installed tools. Click any tool to see its full stats. Tools are always active — no manual equipping needed.
◉
SYSTEM
Operator profile. Four skill bars, achievement tracker, and history of your last 10 missions.

Terminal commands:

helpLists all commands
contractsGo to Contracts screen
marketOpen Black Market
inventoryOpen Inventory
statusPrint level, CC, tools owned
clearWipe terminal output

07MINI-GAMES

During a mission the mini-game panel presents one of two challenges. Completing it grants a breach bonus. Neural Scripting Kit auto-solves both.

CIPHER DECODE

A hacking word is scrambled. Type the correct unscrambled answer and press Enter. Success grants +20% breach bonus.

NODE MAPPING (SYMBOL MATCH)

An 4×2 grid of glyphs — 4 hidden pairs. Tap two tiles to attempt a match. Find all 4 pairs to earn +25% breach bonus. Wrong matches flash red and reset.

// TIMINGTrace fills regardless of the mini-game. Don't linger — a partial bonus beats a trace overload.

10PRO TIPS
  • Buy Neural Kit first (150 CC). Auto-solving puzzles lets you focus entirely on trace and breach bars.
  • Watch the trace meter, not the timer. Trace hitting 100% is the actual failure state. Ghost Proxy Mesh is your best defense.
  • Deploy tools early, not late. Hit "Deploy Proxy" or "Mirror Decoy" when trace hits 50%, not 90%.
  • Ghost Contract is low risk, high variance. No requirements, same trace as ★★★. Run it when you want a mystery payout.
  • Save Zero Day for Crypto Heist. +50% on 550 CC = 825 CC in a single run.
  • Blacklight Scanner pays for itself fast. At 250 CC it reveals bonuses averaging 30–120 CC. Breaks even in 3–4 missions.
  • DarkWire Tap rewards long sessions. Leave the game open — 5 CC/min adds up to 150 CC over 30 minutes for free.
  • Check System screen periodically. Watching your skill bars climb is part of the loop.
