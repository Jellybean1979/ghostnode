01OVERVIEW

GHOSTNODE is a browser-based hacker simulation game. You play as ghost_0x — an anonymous operator working darknet contracts for CipherCoins (CC). None of the hacking is real; all mechanics are thematic simulations designed to feel like the movies.

Your goal is to run contracts, earn CC, spend it on tools in the Black Market, unlock harder contracts, level up, and climb the operator ranks from Novice all the way to Legend.

// NOTE The game auto-saves nothing — progress exists in your current browser session. Refreshing the page resets everything. Plan your run accordingly.
02GAME LOOP

Every session follows the same three-phase loop:

PHASE 1 ACCEPT CONTRACT
→
PHASE 2 RUN MISSION
→
PHASE 3 GET PAID
→
REPEAT BUY TOOLS

Completing contracts earns CipherCoins and XP. XP raises your level and unlocks new rank titles. CC is spent in the Black Market on tools that modify how missions play out — making them faster, safer, or opening up higher-tier contracts.

// TIP Start with easy Recon contracts to stack early CC, then invest in the Ghost Proxy Mesh before attempting anything rated ★★★ or higher.
03THE SCREENS

Use the left sidebar to navigate between five screens. Here's what each one does:

▶
TERMINAL
Sidebar → TERMINAL
Your home base. Boots with a startup sequence and displays ambient system logs. Shows your four core stats at the top. Has a fake command line — type help for a list of commands. Random network activity prints periodically while idle.
◈
CONTRACTS
Sidebar → CONTRACTS
Displays all available contracts as cards. Locked contracts are greyed out — you need the required tools first. The right sidebar shows your current operator stats and trace modifier. Click [ACCEPT CONTRACT] to launch a mission.
⚠
MISSION
Auto-opens on contract start
The live mission screen. Shows the breach progress bar, trace meter, mission countdown timer, and a scrolling terminal log. A mini-game panel appears on the right. You can deploy tools from the bottom bar mid-mission.
◆
BLACK MARKET
Sidebar → BLACK MARKET
The underground tool shop. Browse all available tools, see their effects and costs. Click BUY to purchase — the cost is deducted from your wallet immediately. Owned tools are marked with a green badge.
▣
INVENTORY
Sidebar → INVENTORY
View all installed tools. Click any tool card to see its full stats and effect description in the right panel. Tools are automatically active — no manual equipping required.
◉
SYSTEM
Sidebar → SYSTEM
Your operator profile. Shows the four skill bars (Breach, Stealth, Decrypt, Exploit), your achievement progress, and a history of the last 10 missions with outcomes and earnings.

The Terminal also accepts typed commands:

helpLists all available commands
contractsNavigates to the Contracts screen
marketOpens the Black Market
inventoryOpens your Inventory
statusPrints operator level, CC balance, and tools owned
clearWipes the terminal output
04CURRENCY — CIPHERCOINS (CC)

CipherCoins (CC) is the in-game currency displayed in the top-right corner at all times. You earn CC by completing contracts. You spend CC in the Black Market.

You start with 250 CC — enough to buy the Neural Scripting Kit immediately, or save toward the Ghost Proxy Mesh.

// PASSIVE INCOME If you own the DarkWire Tap, it quietly generates +5 CC every 60 seconds in the background, even while you're on other screens.
// BONUS REWARDS Most contracts have a hidden bonus reward on top of the base payout. Owning the Blacklight Scanner reveals this bonus amount on the contract card — and you earn it too.

07MINI-GAMES

During a mission, the right panel presents one of two mini-games. Completing it grants a bonus breach percentage — speeding up mission completion. If you own the Neural Scripting Kit, this is handled automatically.

CIPHER DECODE
[SUBSYSTEM] Cipher decryption required.
Scrambled:  PTYOCNR
Answer:    ENCRYPT
[RESULT] Correct — breach bonus +20%

A word is scrambled and displayed. Type the correct unscrambled word in the input field and press Enter. The answer is always a hacking-related term. Success grants +20% breach bonus.

NODE MAPPING (SYMBOL MATCH)

A 4×2 grid of symbols is shown (4 pairs, 8 tiles). Click two tiles to attempt a match. Find all 4 pairs to complete the puzzle and earn +25% breach bonus. Wrong matches flash red and reset.

// TIMING The trace meter fills throughout the mission regardless of whether you're playing the mini-game. Don't spend too long on it — a failed mini-game is better than a trace overload.

10PRO TIPS
  • Buy Neural Kit first (150 CC). Auto-solving puzzles means you can focus entirely on the trace and breach bars during missions instead of juggling both.
  • Watch the trace meter, not the timer. The timer is cosmetic pressure — trace hitting 100% is the actual failure state. Ghost Proxy Mesh is your best defense against it.
  • Deploy tool buttons mid-mission. If your trace is climbing fast, don't wait — hit "Deploy Proxy" or "Mirror Decoy" early rather than scrambling at 90%.
  • The Ghost Contract is high variance, not high risk. It has no tool requirements and the same trace rate as a ★★★ contract. Run it when you want a mystery payout.
  • Save the Zero Day for a Crypto Heist. The 50% bonus on top of a 550 CC payout can net you 825 CC in a single run — the best return in the game.
  • Check the System screen after every few missions. Your skill bars and mission history are there — watching them grow is part of the satisfaction loop.
  • Blacklight Scanner pays for itself quickly. At 250 CC, it reveals hidden bonuses averaging 30–120 CC per contract. It breaks even after 3–4 missions.
  • DarkWire Tap is best for long sessions. Leave the game open in a tab and let it drip passive CC. Over 30 minutes that's 150 CC for free.
